## 💡 WHAT IS WELLNESS VALLEY?

Wellness Valley is your intelligent wellness companion that makes healthy living effortless. Simply snap a photo of your meal, and our AI instantly analyzes calories, protein, carbs, and fats. Track your weight with smart OCR technology, log education sessions, and connect with certified wellness coaches - all in one beautiful app.

**Available everywhere you are:** Android, iOS, and Web
