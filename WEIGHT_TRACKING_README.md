# 📊 Weight Tracking with OCR

> Automatically track your weight by taking photos of your weighing scale. Powered by OCR (Optical Character Recognition).

---

## 🎯 What It Does

Take a photo of your weighing scale → OCR extracts the weight value → Saves to your profile → View insights and trends

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 📸 **Camera Capture** - Take photos directly from your phone's camera
- 🤖 **Automatic OCR** - Extract weight values from scale images using AI
- ✏️ **Manual Entry** - Fallback option if OCR fails to detect weight
- ⚖️ **Unit Support** - Track in kilograms (kg) or pounds (lbs)
- 📊 **Statistics** - View latest weight, changes, averages, and trends
- 📅 **History** - Complete timeline of all your weight entries
- 🖼️ **Image Storage** - Review the original photos anytime
- 🗑️ **Delete Option** - Remove entries if needed

---

## 🚀 How to Use

### 1. Open Weight Tracking
Click your profile picture → Select **"Weight Tracking"**

### 2. Take a Photo
- Click **"Take Photo of Weighing Scale"**
- Point camera at your scale's display
- Ensure good lighting and clear numbers
- Tap to capture

### 3. Review & Save
- Wait 2-5 seconds for OCR processing
- Check the detected weight value
- Edit manually if needed
- Select unit (kg or lbs)
- Click **"Save Weight"**

### 4. View Insights
- Automatic redirect to insights page
- See your latest weight
- Check weight change from last entry
- View average, min, and max weights
- Browse complete history with photos

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### For Accurate OCR Detection:

✅ **Good Lighting** - Natural daylight or bright room lighting  
✅ **Steady Camera** - Hold phone steady to avoid blur  
✅ **Clear Display** - Make sure numbers are in focus  
✅ **No Reflections** - Avoid glare on the scale screen  
✅ **Digital Scales** - Work best with LED/LCD displays  
✅ **Close-Up Shot** - Get close enough to see numbers clearly  

### Photo Examples:

**✅ Good Photo:**
- Clear, bright numbers
- No blur or motion
- Centered display
- No shadows

**❌ Poor Photo:**
- Blurry or dark
- Reflections on screen
- Numbers cut off
- Too far away

## ❓ FAQ

### **Q: What if OCR doesn't detect my weight?**
A: Don't worry! You can manually enter your weight. The manual entry field appears automatically if OCR fails.

### **Q: Can I use photos from my gallery?**
A: No, you must take a new photo using the app's camera. This ensures freshness and accuracy.

### **Q: How accurate is the OCR?**
A: About 85-90% success rate with clear, well-lit photos. The system shows a confidence score for each detection.

### **Q: Can I delete wrong entries?**
A: Yes! Click the delete (trash) icon next to any entry in your history.

### **Q: What happens to my photos?**
A: Photos are securely stored in the database and only visible to you. They're kept for reference so you can verify past readings.

### **Q: Does it work with analog scales?**
A: OCR works best with digital scales (LED/LCD displays). Analog scales with needles are harder to read accurately.

### **Q: Can I track multiple people?**
A: Currently, each user account tracks their own weight. Family members need separate accounts.

### **Q: How often should I weigh myself?**
A: Medical experts recommend weighing at the same time daily (typically morning, before eating).

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### OCR Not Detecting Weight

**Problem:** Says "Unable to detect weight"

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Improve lighting on scale display
2. ✅ Hold camera steady (avoid blur)
3. ✅ Get closer to the scale
4. ✅ Clean scale display (remove dust/smudges)
5. ✅ Use manual entry as fallback

---

### Photo Won't Save

**Problem:** "Failed to save weight entry"

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Check internet connection
2. ✅ Try again in a few seconds
3. ✅ Restart the app
4. ✅ Contact support if persists

---

### Weight Not Showing in History

**Problem:** Saved but not visible

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Pull down to refresh the page
2. ✅ Check if entry was deleted accidentally
3. ✅ Sign out and sign back in
4. ✅ Clear browser cache
